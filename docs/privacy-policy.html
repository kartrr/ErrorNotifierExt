Privacy Policy for QA Error Notifier

Last updated: September 01, 2025

Thank you for using QA Error Notifier. This Privacy Policy is to inform you about how we handle data in our browser extension.

Data Collection and Usage

QA Error Notifier is a developer tool designed to capture and display JavaScript errors from web pages. To provide its functionality, the extension processes the following information:

  1. Website Content: The extension captures error messages and stack traces that are generated by the websites you visit.
  2. Web History: The URL of the page where an error occurs is processed to be displayed in the error notification, helping you identify the context of the bug.

IMPORTANT: All of this data is processed locally on your computer. It is never sent to, stored on, or collected by our servers or any third-party services. The data is only used to display notifications to you, the user.

User Settings

The extension uses Chrome's storage (chrome.storage.sync) to save your personal settings, such as error filters and de-duplication preferences. This data is synced with your Google Account and is not accessed by the developer.

Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

We do not transmit any of the above-mentioned data over the network. Your browsing history, website content, and error information remain on your device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
